Biography

I am currently a third-year master student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT), supervised by Prof. Kejing He. I was a research intern at SIAT, Chinese Academy of Sciences from Aug. 2018 to Sep. 2019, supervised by Prof. Min Yang. Before that, I received my B. Eng. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology in 2017.

My research interests mainly lie in Natural Language Processing (NLP), with special emphasis on dialogue generation and document summarization. Recently, I am dedicated to incorporate commonsense knowledge and pre-trained languages models into dialogue generation.
