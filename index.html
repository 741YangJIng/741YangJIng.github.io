Biography

I am a research assistant at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), under the supervision of Prof. Wenjie Li. I received my M.Eng. degree and B.Eng. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology (SCUT) in 2020 and 2017, respectively. Previously, I was a visiting student at SIAT, Chinese Academy of Sciences from Aug. 2018 to Sep. 2019, supervised by Prof. Min Yang.

My research interests mainly lie in Natural Language Processing (NLP), with special emphasis on dialogue systems, natural language generation, and knowledge graph. Recently, I am dedicated to knowledge enhanced dialogue and recommendation oriented dialogue.
